About the project
This is a series of websites about Rebirth & Hope, the first version participated in the exhibition of @bapxao.oi: "Light Up the Burnt Out" in August 2023. Currently, the second version participated in l'officiel O 2024 exhibition: "HOPE - Between Despair & Renewal"
About the artwork
Sowing the Seeds is an interactive installation with the message "Sow the Seeds of Light and Life, Reap the Fruits of Rebirth." Created by @voth000, "Sowing the Seeds" allows viewers to interact with 3D objects that melt and then return to their original shapes, representing the correlation between life and death, the cycle of a human life, being born and dying, falling and rising.
Sowing Seeds
Viewers can plant flowers on the 3D object shining brightly and melting, and move around the artwork, creating random but complete patterns. The work reminds that everyone's life is imperfect and different, but each of us is a special individual, always seeking renewal and rebirth to find our own light.
